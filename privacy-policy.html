Privacy Policy for emovision

Effective Date: June 7, 2025

This app ("emovision") is designed to assist users by analyzing facial videos to detect potential signs of depression. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your data.

1. No Account Required

You do not need to register or log in to use the app.

2. What Data We Collect

The app allows you to upload a facial video for analysis. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII), such as your name, email address, or location.

3. How Your Video Is Processed

Your video is securely transmitted to our own server for real-time analysis.

  • We do not store the video after processing.
  • We do not retain any analysis results.
The data is used solely to perform a one-time emotion or depression detection task.

4. No Data Sharing

We do not share your video or analysis data with any third parties. All processing is done by our own services.

5. Your Control

By uploading your video, you consent to the one-time analysis for mental health screening. You may choose not to use the app if you do not agree with this policy.
